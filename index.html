Keep It Beautiful

Jewelry Care Guide

Simple habits that keep your pieces looking as bright as the day you got them.

By Material

How to Care for Each Piece

Gold

  • Wipe with a soft, lint-free cloth after each wear
  • Clean monthly with warm water and mild soap
  • Store separately to avoid scratching softer metals
  • Remove before swimming, exercising, or cleaning

Silver

  • Polish with a silver cloth to prevent tarnish
  • Store in an anti-tarnish pouch or airtight bag
  • Avoid contact with perfume, lotion, and chlorine
  • Wear it often — silver tarnishes faster when unworn

Diamonds & Gems

  • Soak in warm water with a drop of dish soap
  • Brush gently with a soft, clean toothbrush
  • Rinse thoroughly and pat dry before storing
  • Check prong settings yearly to prevent stone loss

Pearls

  • Wipe with a damp cloth after wearing
  • Apply perfume and hairspray before putting pearls on
  • Store flat and away from harder gems
  • Restring every 2–3 years with regular wear
Pearls are organic and softer than most gems — treat them gently.
Quick Reference

Do's and Don'ts

Do

  • Take jewelry off before showering or sleeping
  • Store pieces separately in soft pouches
  • Bring pieces in for an annual inspection
  • Clean gently after every few wears

Don't

  • Wear jewelry in pools, hot tubs, or the sea
  • Use household cleaners or bleach on any piece
  • Toss pieces together in one drawer
  • Ignore a loose stone or bent prong
FAQ

Care Questions, Answered

Can I get my jewelry professionally cleaned?

Yes — every Horyaal piece includes free lifetime cleaning and inspection at any of our studios.

How often should I have prongs checked?

Once a year for rings worn daily, since prongs slowly wear down and can loosen a stone.

Is it safe to wear jewelry while exercising?

We recommend removing rings and bracelets before workouts to avoid impact damage and bending.

What if a stone falls out?

Stop wearing the piece and contact us right away — repairs are covered under your lifetime warranty.
